## Quick Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **9–10** | Exceptional — professional quality, standout project |
| **7–8** | Very good — strong effort with clear execution |
| **5–6** | Good — solid work, some rough edges |
| **3–4** | Needs work — basic effort, significant gaps |
| **1–2** | Incomplete — minimal effort or didn't demonstrate |

---

> Judges: These are **student teams** building for the first time. Reward clarity, effort, learning, teamwork, and practical thinking — not just advanced code. If a team built something that works and they can explain why it matters, that's a strong signal.
